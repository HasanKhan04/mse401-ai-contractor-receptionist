# Concept Selection

## Concept A: Chatbot Only

Customers interact with an AI chatbot through a website.

Advantages:

* Lowest implementation cost
* Easy deployment
* Handles FAQs

Disadvantages:

* Cannot answer phone calls
* Customers may prefer calling
* Limited lead capture opportunities

## Concept B: Voice FAQ Assistant

Customers call an AI system that answers common questions.

Advantages:

* Supports phone interactions
* Available 24/7

Disadvantages:

* Cannot book appointments
* Limited business value
* Does not fully replace receptionist duties

## Concept C: Contractor-Focused AI Receptionist

Customers call an AI receptionist capable of answering questions, collecting customer information, identifying service requests, and scheduling appointments.

Advantages:

* Addresses the full workflow
* Improves lead capture
* Reduces administrative workload
* Available 24/7

Disadvantages:

* More complex to implement

## Concept Evaluation

| Criteria               | Chatbot | Voice FAQ Assistant | AI Receptionist |
| ---------------------- | ------- | ------------------- | --------------- |
| Customer Accessibility | Medium  | High                | High            |
| Appointment Scheduling | Low     | Low                 | High            |
| Lead Capture           | Medium  | Medium              | High            |
| Automation             | Medium  | Medium              | High            |
| Business Value         | Medium  | Medium              | High            |

## Selected Concept

Concept C was selected because it provides the greatest business value while addressing the complete customer interaction process for home service businesses.
