# Concept Selection

Following the problem analysis, three potential concepts were generated to address the issue of missed calls and inefficient customer communication for small home service businesses. The concepts were evaluated based on their ability to improve customer accessibility, automate business processes, capture leads, support appointment scheduling, and provide overall business value.

## Concept A: Chatbot Only

This concept consists of a website-based AI chatbot that allows customers to ask questions and receive information through a text interface.

Advantages:

* Lowest implementation cost
* Simple deployment
* Effective for answering frequently asked questions
* Can provide information outside business hours

Disadvantages:

* Cannot answer phone calls
* Requires customers to visit a website
* Many customers prefer calling service businesses directly
* Limited lead capture opportunities

## Concept B: Voice FAQ Assistant

This concept consists of a voice-based AI system capable of answering incoming calls and responding to common customer questions.

Advantages:

* Supports phone interactions
* Available 24/7
* Reduces repetitive customer inquiries
* More accessible than a website chatbot

Disadvantages:

* Cannot schedule appointments
* Limited ability to collect customer information
* Does not fully replace receptionist responsibilities
* Limited business value beyond answering questions

## Concept C: Contractor-Focused AI Receptionist

This concept consists of an AI receptionist specifically designed for small home service businesses. The system can answer incoming calls, collect customer information, identify service requests, answer common questions, and schedule appointments automatically.

Advantages:

* Addresses the complete customer interaction workflow
* Improves lead capture
* Reduces administrative workload
* Supports appointment scheduling
* Available 24/7
* Designed specifically for contractor businesses

Disadvantages:

* More complex to implement
* Requires integration with scheduling systems
* Requires business-specific configuration

## Concept Evaluation

The concepts were evaluated using a weighted comparison of key project objectives. The evaluation criteria were selected based on the needs identified during the problem analysis and stakeholder assessment.

| Criteria               | Chatbot Only | Voice FAQ Assistant | Contractor-Focused AI Receptionist |
| ---------------------- | ------------ | ------------------- | ---------------------------------- |
| Customer Accessibility | Medium       | High                | High                               |
| Appointment Scheduling | Low          | Low                 | High                               |
| Lead Capture           | Medium       | Medium              | High                               |
| Automation             | Medium       | Medium              | High                               |
| Business Value         | Medium       | Medium              | High                               |
| Contractor Specificity | Low          | Low                 | High                               |

## Selected Concept

Concept C was selected because it best addresses the needs identified during the problem analysis. Unlike the chatbot-only solution, it supports phone-based interactions, which are commonly preferred by customers contacting home service businesses. Unlike the voice FAQ assistant, it supports appointment scheduling and customer information collection, enabling it to perform the primary responsibilities of a receptionist.

By combining call answering, lead capture, FAQ handling, and appointment scheduling into a single solution, the contractor-focused AI receptionist provides the greatest overall value to both businesses and customers while remaining aligned with the project objectives.
