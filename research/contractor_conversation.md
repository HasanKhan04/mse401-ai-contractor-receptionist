## Contractor Conversation

Date: June 2026

Method: Phone conversation with a local contractor

Discussion Notes:

* Most calls come from potential customers requesting quotes or service information.
* Calls are frequently missed while working on job sites or driving between appointments.
* Returning missed calls can take several hours, and some customers may contact competitors instead.
* Common information collected from customers includes:

  * Name
  * Phone number
  * Service requested
  * Property location
  * Preferred appointment date
* Having someone available to answer calls during busy periods would improve customer service.
* After-hours calls are often missed entirely.
* Cost is an important consideration for small contractor businesses.
* They do not have time to manually handle all this themselves

Takeaways:

* Immediate call answering is important for lead capture.
* Appointment booking should be supported.
* The solution should be affordable for small businesses.
* The solution should operate outside normal business hours.
* The solution should cause no extra work as they are already so busy.
