
# Problem Analysis

Small home service businesses such as plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, landscapers, and contractors frequently miss customer calls because owners and technicians spend much of their time working on job sites. When calls are missed, potential customers often contact competitors instead.

Many small businesses cannot afford a dedicated receptionist or 24/7 phone coverage. As a result, customer inquiries are frequently directed to voicemail, creating delays in communication and reducing customer satisfaction.

The primary stakeholders affected by this problem are business owners, field technicians, office staff, and customers. Business owners may lose revenue from missed opportunities, while customers experience delays in receiving assistance or booking appointments.

Current solutions include human receptionists, voicemail systems, and generic AI receptionists. Human receptionists can be expensive, voicemail systems do not provide immediate assistance, and generic AI receptionists are often not tailored to the needs of home service businesses.

This project proposes a contractor-focused AI receptionist capable of answering calls, collecting customer information, answering common questions, and scheduling appointments automatically. The solution aims to improve customer experience while reducing administrative workload for small businesses.
