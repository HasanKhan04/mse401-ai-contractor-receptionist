Concept 1: Voicemail and Call Transcription System

Description:
A system that records missed calls, transcribes voicemails, and forwards messages to contractors through email or SMS.

Pros:

* Low implementation cost
* Simple to deploy
* Minimal setup required

Cons:

* Does not provide immediate assistance to customers
* Customers may not leave voicemails
* Delays response time for urgent inquiries

Decision:
Rejected because it does not adequately address the core problem of missed opportunities caused by unanswered calls.


Concept 2: Outsourced Human Virtual Receptionist Service

Description:
A third-party receptionist service that answers calls on behalf of contractors and forwards customer information.

Pros:

* Human interaction
* Capable of handling a variety of customer inquiries
* Familiar solution already used by some businesses

Cons:

* Higher ongoing operating costs
* Limited scalability as call volume increases
* Service quality may vary between operators

Decision:
Rejected because recurring costs make the solution less accessible for small contractor businesses, and scaling requires additional staffing.



Selected Concept: AI Contractor Receptionist

Description:
An AI-powered voice receptionist that answers inbound calls, responds to common customer inquiries, captures lead information, and books appointments.

This concept provided the best balance of cost, availability, scalability, and functionality. Unlike voicemail systems, it can interact with callers in real time, and unlike outsourced receptionist services, it can scale without significant increases in operating costs.
